Embedded Project

We’re bringing a Bluetooth-controlled 4WD robot car to life, complete with an articulated mechanical arm. Our project features a mobile base driven by four geared motors and an arm utilizing four specific servos for its Base, Shoulder, Elbow, and Wrist. By managing our Arduino Uno and motor shield via laptop, we’ve collaboratively assembled the chassis and are now fine-tuning the code to make it fully functional.

IT Era

This is my adobe Photoshop designed for our IT era project Bea's Seafoods Kitchen. The layout uses a black and pink background with a diagonal split, giving it a modern, eye‑catching look. There are three food images: a black pot of mixed seafood dish on the left, a white bowl of seafood soup in the center, and a pink plate of grilled seafood on the right. The top and bottom feature the Bea's Kitchen logo with a crab illustration. The pink text ‘YOUR FAVOURITE SEA FOODS HERE!’ curves around the center, while ‘BEA'S SEAFOODS KITCHEN’ is boldly placed below the left image. Contact information and a barcode are positioned on the right for product packaging.
